Downloading Content

DIKSHA has a lot of content. You can download any DIKSHA content when you are connected to the Internet. . All files downloaded from DIKSHA are saved on your computer, laptop, mobile or any other device as .ecar files. That is, all the downloaded files have an extension .ecar.

This section tells you how to download files from DIKSHA

Must Do

Before downloading content from DIKSHA, ensure the following.

  • You are connected to the internet.
  • You have DIKSHA offline consumption application installed on your desktop.
